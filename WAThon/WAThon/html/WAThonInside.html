The Walk-a-thon!

Join us at ROFEH International's first-ever Walk For ROFEH on Sunday morning April 28th.
We will be walking at the beautiful DCR Chestnut Hill Reservoir to raise funds for patients and families from around the world and throughout the USA as they leave their homes to seek emergency medical treatment at Boston's most prestigious medical centers.
ROFEH International's vital support services help to create a home away from home and allows family to do what is most important: focus on the patient.
